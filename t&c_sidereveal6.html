🏆 Tournament Overview

Welcome to the Tri Series Cricket Tournament, where three cricketing powerhouses — India, Australia, and New Zealand — compete in an unforgettable battle for supremacy. This special event brings together passionate cricket fans and competitive players in a thrilling challenge where every point counts and every match can change the course of the leaderboard.

Players can select their favorite nation and contribute toward their team's overall success through eligible gameplay activities. Whether you support India's Blue Team, Australia's Gold Team, or New Zealand's Black Caps, your participation directly impacts your team's position in the competition.

Throughout the Tournament, players will earn points, rise through the rankings, and compete against supporters of rival nations. Team scores will continuously update as players contribute points, creating an intense and competitive environment where consistency, dedication, and performance are rewarded.

The live leaderboard allows participants to track both individual and team progress throughout the event. Rankings may shift rapidly as teams battle for the top position, ensuring excitement from the opening day until the final moments of the Tournament.

In addition to national pride and leaderboard recognition, players will have the opportunity to earn exciting rewards based on participation and performance. Special prizes may be awarded to top-performing players and members of the winning team following Tournament completion and verification.

Gather your determination, support your nation, and prepare for an unforgettable cricket experience. Help your team climb the rankings, earn valuable rewards, and become part of the journey toward crowning the ultimate Tri Series Champion.
