🏅 Tournament Overview

Welcome to the Red, White & Blue Tournament, an exciting team-based competition where players battle for supremacy, rewards, and leaderboard glory. Choose your side and contribute points through eligible gameplay activities to help your team rise above the competition.

Three teams enter the arena — Red Team, White Team, and Blue Team — each competing for the top position on the leaderboard. Every spin, every point, and every contribution helps your team move closer to victory.

Players earn Tournament points throughout the event and contribute toward both their individual ranking and their team's overall standing. As the competition intensifies, leaderboard positions may change rapidly, creating excitement from start to finish.

The live leaderboard allows participants to track progress in real time, compare team performance, and see which side is leading the race toward championship glory.

In addition to team pride and leaderboard recognition, players may have the opportunity to earn exclusive rewards based on participation, ranking, and overall Tournament performance. Special prizes may be awarded to top-performing players and members of the winning team following Tournament completion and verification.

Choose your color, join the competition, and prove your team's strength. Work together, climb the rankings, and help crown the ultimate champion of the Red, White & Blue Tournament.
